Rivet × Crewline — How the Whole Thing Works

The complete picture on one page, in plain language. (July 2026)

1 · The People

Three kinds of people use the same website — on a phone or a computer. It can even be installed like an app.

🛠️ Workers

Create a Work Card (their skills, licenses & work history), get matched to real jobs nearby, and apply with one tap.

🏗️ Employers

Post jobs, instantly see matched & verified workers, chat with them, schedule interviews, and hire.

🛡️ Admin (you)

A private control room at /admin: approve workers' license photos, look up users, and remove bad job posts.

everyone signs in first — with an email code, Google, or a text-message code

2 · The Website — one server on Render does everything

There is no separate "frontend" and "backend" to maintain — one program draws every page AND does all the thinking. It lives at rivet-crewline.onrender.com and restarts itself if it ever crashes.

3 · What That Server Does (its six jobs)

Think of it as six departments inside one building.

🔐 The Front Desk

Sign-in & safety. Checks the email code / Google / phone code, and blocks abuse: wrong-password lockouts, code limits, and protection against fake websites posting forms here.

⚙️ The Matchmaker

Scores every worker-and-job pair: right trade? pay meets their minimum? close enough to home? licenses verified? Higher score = shown first, to both sides.

📥 The Job Collector

Every 6 hours it visits ~40 big employers' career sites (Intel, hospitals, manufacturers…) and brings back 23,000+ real openings, removing stale or duplicate ones.

🔔 The Messenger

When anything happens — a message, an application update, a new matching job — it tells the person everywhere: inside the site, phone notification, email, and text message.

🛡️ The Verification Desk

A worker photographs their license → it waits in the admin's queue → the admin approves it → the worker earns the “Verified” badge, which genuinely boosts their match scores. This is the trust engine.

📋 The Hiring Desk

Application pipelines for employers (like moving cards on a board), shift postings workers can claim, interview scheduling with calendar invites, and two-way reviews after the job.

everything gets saved, instantly and permanently

4 · The Memory — Turso database

One secure database remembers it all: accounts, Work Cards, credentials & their proofs, all 23,000+ jobs, applications, messages, shifts, reviews, and notification subscriptions. Nothing is lost when the site updates or restarts. Passwords are stored scrambled — even we can't read them.

a few trusted outside services help with specific tasks

5 · The Outside Helpers

✉️ Brevo

Delivers our emails: the 6-digit sign-up codes, welcome emails, and job alerts.

💬 Twilio

Sends the text messages: phone sign-in codes and urgent job alerts.

🔔 Google/Mozilla Push

Carries phone & desktop notifications — they arrive even when the site is closed.

🔑 Google Sign-In

The “Continue with Google” button — Google vouches for the person's email.

🤖 Groq AI

Writing help: drafts worker bios, suggests interview questions, translates the site to Spanish.

🏢 Employer career sites

The source of the real job listings the Collector gathers every 6 hours.

6 · Two Journeys, Start to Finish

How it all connects, told as a story.

🛠️ A worker's journey

Sign up→ Enter the 6-digit code from email→ Build Work Card→ Upload license photo→ Admin approves ✓ Verified badge→ See matched real jobs→ Apply in one tap→ Chat & interview (calendar invite)→ Hired 🎉

🏗️ An employer's journey

Sign up→ Post a job→ Matched, verified workers appear instantly→ Nearby workers get alerted automatically→ Review · chat · interview→ Hire & leave a review
